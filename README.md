A hypothetical model of emergent gravity through brane coupling in higher dimensions.

## Abstract

This work presents a hypothesis where gravity phenomena and the existence of mass emerge from fundamental interactions occurring in 5-dimensional spacetime (CP5D). We postulate the existence of two coupled branes: the matter brane (B‚Çò‚Çê) and the antimatter brane (B‚Çê‚Çô), whose mutual gravitational repulsion tensions the B‚Çò‚Çê brane, providing it with a global gravitational background.
